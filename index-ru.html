MINOS is our hotel’s remote restaurant and beach club with a wide deck over water and a private cabana. Bay
Yalikavak

Yalikavak bay lies in the northwestern part of the peninsula. Several decades ago, it was a simple, small fishing village only 19 km from the center of Bodrum.

Today Yalikavak is one of the fastest growing areas of Bodrum peninsula. Not only does it attract a large number of local residents who enjoy developed infrastructure and higher standard of living but also invesCtors who see the potential of growth of this area.

Undoubtedly, main reason for this development is the presence of the second largest marina in Europe. Palmarina Yalikavak is distinctively designed for mega yachts up to 150 meters in size. Besides state of the art facilities for the yachts and yacht owners, you will also find numerous luxury facilities such as spa & fitness, exclusive boutiques and designer stores, a kids fun water park, a deluxe supermarket and a variety of fine-dine restaurants and classy cafes serving Turkish, European and Asian cuisine. Some of the world-famous restaurant brands are residing here for example Cipriani, Nobu and the Billionaires Club.
